Our Story

CEIDE (Creative Education for Interactive Digital Experiences) is a vibrant learning platform dedicated to 3D animation, game design, VFX, and digital art. Founded by a team of industry professionals, CEIDE was born out of a need to bridge the gap between education and the digital media industry. We believe that high-quality education should be accessible, inspiring, and built for real-world success.
